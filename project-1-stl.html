Tution
Tute Cover Design

This project focused on designing a compelling and professional tutorial (tute) cover for an academic institution, blending subject-specific visuals with clean, modern design. The aim was to create a cover that not only reflects the essence of the subject but also aligns with the institution’s branding. Using tools like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I incorporated symbolic elements, bold typography, and balanced layouts to ensure clarity and engagement. The final design elevated the look of the tutorial booklet, making it both functional and visually appealing for students and educators alike.
